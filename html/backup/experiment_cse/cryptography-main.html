Welcome to the Cryptography lab.In this lab, we will do virtual experiments to understand the basic mathematical foundations of cryptography,to gain insightful experience by working with fundamental cryptographic applications and to train in the art of design and analysis of information security protocols.

   Breaking the Shift Cipher

   Breaking the Mono-alphabetic Substitution Cipher

   One-Time Pad and Perfect Secrecy

   Message Authentication Codes

   Cryptographic Hash Functions and Applications

   Symmetric Key Encryption Standards (DES)

   Symmetric Key Encryption Standards (AES)

The experiments are targeted at students who have just finished Senior undergraduate/post-graduate students. This laboratory is aligned with an introductory course on Cryptography.

The pre-requisites for this course are :

  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Algorithms
